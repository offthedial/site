Hey everyone.

With the new season of IDTGA there have been some changes to the Master Division. Below are the minimum skill floor to be in the Master Division:

- LUTI Div 5 (most recent season)

- DSB / OCE Div 1 (most recent season)

- 2x Top 2-4 tournament results (past 3 months regular, 6 months majors)

  - 25 teams or more

  - Open event, no skill cap

- X2700 Takoroka (past 3 months peak)

- X3000 Tentatek (past 3 months peak)

- Win Power Bracket twice (past 6 months)

- Come Top 4 Master Bracket (past 3 months)

If you believe you are eligible for the Master Division, please make sure to say so in the competitive experience so we don't miss you accidently (you results will be verified accordingly).
